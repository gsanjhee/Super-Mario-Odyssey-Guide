Cap Kindom

Cap Kingdom is the first Kingdom you can explore in the game, and where you first meet Cappy in you adventure. No power moons needed to leave this Kingdom. Total of 17 power moons are available in the Kingdom.

Cascade Kingdom

Second Kingdom in the game, and where you first find and restore the Odyssey. 5 power moons are required in order to leave the Kindgom and a total of 25 moons ar available.

Sand Kingdom

Third Kingdom you can ewxplore in the game. It's one of the largest Kingdoms in the whole odyssey where you have to fight both broodal and non-broodal boss fights. 16 power moons are needed to leave this Kingdom whereas, 69 power moons are available in total.

Lake Kingdom

The first kingdom in the path fork you get to choose between. Either the fourth or fifth Kingdom you can explore in the game and includes another Broodal fight. 8 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom whereas, 33 power moons are available.

Wooded Kingdom

Wooded Kingdom is either the fourth or fifth Kingdom you can explore in game (in addition to the Lake Kingdom), and is one of the larger sized Kingdoms. Here you can face both Broodal and non-Broodal bosses. 16 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and 54 are available.

Cloud Kingdom

Cloud Kingdom is the sixth Kingdom you can explore in game, and serves as a small side kingdom used for your first confrontation with Bowser. No Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom but 2 base power moons are available.

Lost Kingdom

Lost Kingdom is the seventh Kingdom you can explore in game, and is where you must once again restore and repair the Odyssey. 10 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and 25 base moons are available.

Metro Kingdom

Metro Kingdom is the eighth Kingdom you can explore in game and is one of the larger sized Kingdoms. Here youcan face both Broodal and non-Broodal bosses. 20 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and a total of 66 base power moons are available. After completing this level, you get to go the traditional festival organised my Mayor Pauline and get to finish a couple easy mini-levels in block form.

Snown Kingdom

Snow Kingdom is the ninth or tenth Kingdom you can explore in game (in addition to the Seaside Kingdom), and includes another Broodal fight. 10 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom. A total of 37 base power moons are available.

Seaside Kingdom

Seaside Kingdom is the ninth or tenth Kingdom you can explore in game (in addition to the Snow Kingdom), and is one of the larger sized Kingdoms. Here you can face both Broodal and non-Broodal bosses. 10 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom. A total of 57 base power moons are available.

Luncheon Kingdom

Luncheon Kingdom is the eleventh Kingdom, and is one of the larger sized Kingdoms. Here you can face both Broodal and non-Broodal bosses. 18 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and 57 base moons are available.

Ruined Kingdom

Ruined Kingdom is the twelfth Kingdom you can explore in game, and serves as a small side kingdom used for a fight against a formidable boss. 3 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and 5 base power moons are available.

Bowser's Castle

Bowser's Kingdom is the thirteenth Kingdom you can explore in game, and is one of the larger sized Kingdoms - where you take on a host of Bosses. 8 Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom and 45 base Power Moons are available.

Moon Kingdom

Moon Kingdom is the final kingdom to explore as part of the main story, and is where you will face the final boss. No Power Moons are needed to leave this Kingdom, as it serves as a bridge from the end of the main story to Post Game content however a total of 27 base Power Moons are available.

Mushroom Kingdom

Mushroom Kingdom is the first of three secret Kingdoms you can explore after completing the main story. It is one of the larger sized Kingdoms and can be unlocked by completing the main story. Unlike other Kingdoms, it has now Moon Rock to reveal more Power Moons, but Archivist Toadette will reward achievements with Power Moons. There are a total of 43 Power Moons available.

Dark Side

Dark Side is the second of three secret Kingdoms you can explore after completing the main story. While not a true Kingdom, this unlockable area will be made available on the Moon after obtaining 250 Power Moons total. Total of 24 Power Moons are available at the Kingdom.

Darker Side

Darker Side is the final secret Kingdom you can explore after completing the main story. While not a true Kingdom like the Dark Side, this unlockable area will be made available on the Moon after obtaining 500 Power Moons total. Only 1 Power Moon availabe.
